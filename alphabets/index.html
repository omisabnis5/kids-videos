ABC Tracing Time! 🖍️

Watch the crayon draw each big letter — then trace it in the air with your finger!

Ready to learn your ABCs?
A friendly crayon will show you how to write every big letter, one line at a time.

💡 For grown-ups: the numbers show the stroke order. Pause on any letter and let your child trace it in the air, on your palm, or on paper with a chunky crayon. Little hands learn best with big, happy movements!
